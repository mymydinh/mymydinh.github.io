Process

From brief to release

Every feature I touch starts with a project brief. I gather requirements from stakeholders, align on business goals, and translate those into clear documentation that guides development and design teams through the sprint process.

Working in Jira, I track each sprint's progress, flag dependencies, and help coordinate cross-functional handoffs — making sure content, design, and engineering are all moving in sync toward release.
