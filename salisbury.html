Overview

Building a brand around
women's empowerment

Salisbury Plastic Surgery came to us looking to establish a real online presence — a brand voice that resonated with their audience and a social strategy that would drive both awareness and in-clinic sales. Starting from scratch, we built a full identity around the themes of beauty, confidence, and women's empowerment.

From creating the content calendar to shooting and editing photos, writing copy, and designing every promotional graphic — this was a true full-service engagement that touched every part of their digital presence.
